DeepFake Detection Project

Python


DeepFake videos are AI generated doctored videos with the potential to spread false information, most often for malicious purposes. This summer I worked on designing a system to classify DeepFake videos as real or fake, using a video magnification method known as Eulerian Video Magnification. This allowed for discrepencies in fake videos to be amplified and tracked for the best results possible.
